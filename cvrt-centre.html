RSA Approved

Commercial Vehicle Testing

Our CVRT centre is fully approved by the Road Safety Authority of Ireland. With 2 dedicated test lanes and an experienced team of accredited testers, we provide fast and professional vehicle testing.

  • RSA approved 2 CVRT test lanes
  • CVRT pre check and voluntary checks
  • Late evening and Saturday testing
  • Tacho calibrations, weight plating and speed limiter calibrations

Services

What We Offer

Our fully equipped test centre handles all standard commercial vehicle testing requirements, with flexible scheduling to minimise disruption to your fleet.

CVRT Testing

Full RSA commercial vehicle roadworthiness tests across 2 dedicated test lanes for HGVs, trailers, buses, and light commercial vehicles.

Pre-Test Checks

Thorough pre-CVRT inspections and voluntary checks to identify potential failures before the official test.

Tacho Calibrations

Digital and analogue tachograph calibration by trained and certified technicians, compliant with EU regulations.

Weight Plating & Speed Limiters

Authorised weight plating and speed limiter calibrations for commercial vehicles in line with Irish and EU requirements.

Evening & Saturday Testing

Late evening and Saturday testing slots available to keep your fleet compliant without disrupting your working week.

Fleet Bookings

We accommodate fleet bookings for multiple vehicles. Contact us to arrange a schedule that works for your operation.
