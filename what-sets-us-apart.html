Eight Reasons to Choose STL

With over 50 years' experience STL is a market leader in 3PL Logistics. Here's what makes us the trusted choice for multinationals and national companies alike.

01

Track Record

With over 50 years' experience STL is a market leader in 3PL Logistics and a proven partner with a deep history of reliable delivery.

02

Management

Highly experienced management team in place with some members bringing over 40 years of logistics expertise to every decision.

03

Infrastructure

Significant infrastructure and ability to scale up, with 1.3M sq.ft of warehousing, 150+ trucks and 400+ trailers across 8 locations.

04

Brand

Established brand in the market with a robust service offering. A host of multinational clients trust STL to deliver every day.

Accreditation

Fully accredited in ISO 9001 and ISO 45001. We also hold HPRA, SEDEX, and CDP accreditations. Compliance is built into everything we do.

06

Service

Customer service is critical to our daily ethos. We exist to make our clients' supply chains work, and we take that personally.

07

Integration

Experience of IT integrations between SAP and our Warehouse Management System, Mandata, for seamless data flow with your systems.

08

Strategic Partner

Multiple strategic partner with customs expertise and ability to source container slots to the Far East, making us a true end-to-end logistics partner.
