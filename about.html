About STL

Founded in Limerick in 1973

The company was founded in 1973 in Limerick and is headquartered from a 120,000 sq.ft warehouse on a 16 acre site in Annacotty Business Park, Limerick.

STL operate from numerous depots around Ireland including Limerick, Tipperary, Clare, Dublin, Roscommon and Belfast. STL have over 1.3 million sq.ft of warehousing throughout the group, with a fleet of approximately 150 trucks and 400 trailers.

Our warehousing and fleet service customers from the Beverage, Pharmaceutical, Medical Device, FMCG, Agricultural and Retail industries.

Leading Edge Integrated Logistics

STL Logistics is one of the leading third party logistics providers in Ireland, operating throughout Ireland & the U.K. for over 50 years.

We provide leading edge integrated logistics solutions for multinational and national companies. We provide supply chain solutions designed and customised to achieve maximum flexibility and agility.

Our 1.3M sq.ft of warehousing and fleet of over 150 trucks throughout the Island of Ireland service customers from the Beverage, Pharmaceutical, Medical Device, FMCG, Agricultural and Retail industries.
