Warehousing Services

Comprehensive Storage & Logistics

STL provides a full suite of warehousing services tailored to the needs of each client, from high-volume FMCG storage to pharmaceutical-grade product handling. Our team and systems are built to deliver accuracy, compliance and efficiency.

  • Warehousing of products including receipt and dispatch management
  • Additional value add services such as handball, labelling, repalletisation, de-topping, temperature monitoring etc.
  • Compile a suite of management reports for Customers as required
  • Maintain stock records including cycle counting and stock takes
  • Develop and operate IT Solution(s) as appropriate to support the business
  • Segregate, quarantine and report delivery of unapproved Products
  • Specific case picking, at a case, inner and each level
